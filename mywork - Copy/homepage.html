*SegreGO* is an innovative and user-centric platform that transforms the landscape of waste management by formalizing informal waste sectors, educating users, and motivating them through an interactive interface. It not only helps create a cleaner environment but also provides opportunities for economic empowerment and sustainable practices.






SERVICES

AI Chatbot

AI chatbot is a user interactive tool of our webpage that answers to the questions by the user and also saves data entered by the user to update the company about garbage dumped at open areas.

Waste segregation

Explaining the users about different types of wastes, the need of waste segregation and the steps to adopt waste segregation.

Product Sales

Showcase recycled products in an attractive and user-friendly online store. Use eco-friendly packaging materials for sustainable product delivery.

Formalizing the Informal sector

Registration of the informal waste workers would not only help them to get some value for their work but teaching them about correct practices of waste segregation would help to manage waste in a better way.

Eco Nidhi

Eco Nidhi is a donation page where the users can contribute directly to the different moral activities.
